Heavy 1

The Next Generation Electric Agricultural Aircraft

Home / Civilian / Heavy 1

Purpose-Built for Agriculture

The Heavy 1 is Edison Aerospace's flagship electric agricultural aircraft, engineered from the ground up to transform how farmers protect and manage their crops. Powered by 8 independent electric motors, the Heavy 1 delivers unmatched redundancy and safety -- if any single motor fails, the aircraft continues flying without interruption.

With no fossil fuels on board, the Heavy 1 eliminates the fire risk that has plagued traditional ag aviation for decades. Crop dusting operations near dry fields and volatile chemicals demand the safest possible platform, and an all-electric powerplant delivers exactly that.

Beyond safety, the Heavy 1 offers significantly lower operating costs compared to turbine-powered ag planes. No avgas, no turbine overhauls, no oil changes. Electric motors require virtually zero maintenance, translating to more uptime and more acres covered per season.

Technical Specifications

Wingspan 40 feet
Maximum Takeoff Weight (MTOW) 3,000 lbs
Hopper Capacity 200 gallons
Takeoff Distance 300 feet (STOL)
Cruise Speed 90 knots
Powerplant 8 Electric Motors
Range Optimized for agricultural operations
Payload 1,200+ lbs

Key Features

Electric Propulsion

8 independent electric motors provide unmatched redundancy. If any motor fails, the aircraft continues operating safely without loss of control.

STOL Capability

Operates from strips as short as 300 feet, allowing takeoff and landing from unprepared surfaces right next to the fields being treated.

200-Gallon Tank

Large hopper capacity means fewer refills per field, maximizing productivity and minimizing downtime during critical spray windows.

Precision Application

GPS-guided precision spraying ensures accurate chemical application, reducing waste and environmental impact while improving crop coverage.

Lower Costs

53% reduction in total operating costs compared to traditional turbine ag aircraft. No fuel, no engine overhauls, minimal maintenance.

Zero Emissions

Clean, quiet electric operation with zero exhaust emissions. Ideal for operations near communities, livestock, and sensitive ecosystems.
