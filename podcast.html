Electric Horizons

A Podcast by Edison Aerospace

About the Podcast

Electric Horizons explores the cutting edge of electric aviation, agricultural technology, and the future of aerospace. Join Edison Aerospace as we discuss industry trends, interview innovators, and share our journey developing the next generation of electric aircraft.

Each episode brings you closer to the people, technology, and ideas shaping the future of flight. From engineering breakthroughs to the economics of electric agriculture, no topic is off limits.

The Economics of Electric Agriculture

We break down the real-world cost savings and ROI of electric agricultural aircraft. From fuel elimination to reduced maintenance cycles, learn why operators are making the switch and what the numbers actually look like over a five-year ownership period.

Safety First -- Why Electric Matters

A deep dive into the safety advantages of electric propulsion for agricultural aviation. We examine crash survivability data, the dangers of aviation fuel fires, and how eight independent electric motors provide redundancy that traditional single-engine aircraft simply cannot match.
