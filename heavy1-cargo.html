Heavy 1 Cargo

Electric Unmanned Cargo Delivery

Home / Civilian / Heavy 1 Cargo

Purpose-Built for Cargo Delivery

Based on the proven Heavy 1 airframe, the Heavy 1 Cargo variant is purpose-built for autonomous civilian cargo delivery. It replaces the agricultural spray system with a modular cargo bay, retaining all the advantages of electric propulsion: 8 redundant motors, STOL capability, low operating costs, and zero emissions.

Ideal for remote area supply, medical supply delivery, humanitarian aid, and inter-island logistics, the Heavy 1 Cargo opens new possibilities for cargo operations where traditional aircraft and ground transportation fall short.

Technical Specifications

Wingspan 40 feet
Maximum Takeoff Weight (MTOW) 3,000 lbs
Cargo Bay Capacity 1,200+ lbs
Takeoff Distance 300 feet (STOL)
Cruise Speed 90 knots
Powerplant 8 Electric Motors
Operations Fully autonomous, remotely piloted, or hybrid
Range Optimized for regional cargo delivery

Use Cases

Remote Area Supply

Deliver supplies to locations without road access or runways. The Heavy 1 Cargo's STOL capability allows operations from unprepared surfaces in the most remote areas.

Medical Delivery

Time-critical medical supplies, organs, and blood products delivered autonomously. Every minute counts, and the Heavy 1 Cargo operates 24/7 without pilot fatigue.

Humanitarian Aid

Disaster relief cargo delivery where infrastructure is damaged or destroyed. Operate independently of roads, bridges, and conventional airport infrastructure.

Island Logistics

Inter-island cargo service with STOL capability. Connect island communities with reliable, low-cost autonomous cargo flights without requiring full-length runways.

Agricultural Supply

Deliver seeds, fertilizer, and equipment to remote farms. Complement the agricultural Heavy 1 by keeping farms supplied without requiring ground logistics.

E-Commerce Last Mile

Autonomous delivery for high-value or time-sensitive packages. Bypass ground congestion and reach customers faster with direct aerial delivery.

Advantages Over Traditional Cargo

No Pilot Required

Lower costs and 24/7 operations. Eliminate pilot scheduling, training costs, and fatigue limitations for round-the-clock cargo delivery.

STOL Capability

No runway needed -- operate from 300ft strips, dirt roads, fields, or any cleared area. Access locations that conventional cargo aircraft cannot reach.

53% Lower Operating Costs

Electric propulsion eliminates fuel, engine overhauls, and oil changes. Dramatically lower cost-per-mile compared to turbine-powered cargo aircraft.

Zero Emissions

Regulatory advantage with clean, quiet electric operation. Meet sustainability mandates and operate in emissions-restricted zones.

Autonomous Operations

Consistent, reliable operations without human variability. Pre-programmed routes, automated takeoff and landing, and real-time monitoring.

Ground Control Station Integration

The Heavy 1 Cargo operates seamlessly with Edison Aerospace's Ground Control Station (GCS) platform. Monitor cargo missions in real time, manage multiple aircraft simultaneously, and maintain full situational awareness from a single operator console.

The GCS provides flight planning, payload management, real-time telemetry, and automated contingency procedures -- everything needed to run a scalable autonomous cargo operation.
