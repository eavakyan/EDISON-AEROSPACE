Safety, Security & Legal

Comprehensive protection built into every aspect of the Heavy 1 platform

Built for Safety From the Ground Up

Edison Aerospace has designed the Heavy 1 with safety as the foundational principle. Our electric propulsion system eliminates many of the risks inherent in traditional agricultural aviation while meeting or exceeding all regulatory requirements.

Safety, Security, and Legal Categories

Safe

  • 8 independent electric motors -- loss of any single motor does not compromise flight
  • No flammable aviation fuel -- eliminates the #1 cause of post-crash fatalities
  • Distributed electric propulsion provides inherent redundancy
  • Advanced flight control systems with automatic stabilization
  • Emergency autorotation and controlled descent capability
  • Reduced noise profile for safer operations near populated areas

Secure

  • Encrypted command and control links
  • GPS-independent navigation capability
  • Geofencing and no-fly zone compliance
  • Real-time telemetry monitoring
  • Tamper-resistant flight control systems
  • Comprehensive data logging and black box

Legal

  • FAA Part 137 agricultural aircraft operator compliance
  • Working with FAA on electric aircraft certification pathway
  • Full compliance with state and federal pesticide application regulations
  • Pilot licensing and training requirements met
  • Insurance requirements satisfied with reduced premiums
  • Environmental compliance -- zero emissions, reduced noise

Why Electric Is Safer

Agricultural aviation has one of the highest accident rates in commercial aviation. The Heavy 1's electric architecture addresses the root causes of these incidents.

No Fire Risk

Elimination of flammable Jet-A fuel removes the primary cause of fatal outcomes in agricultural aviation accidents.

Redundant Propulsion

Eight independent motors ensure continued flight even with multiple motor failures, unlike single-engine turbine aircraft.

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous telemetry provides ground operators with full visibility into aircraft health, position, and performance metrics.

Encrypted Communications

Military-grade encryption on all command, control, and telemetry links prevents unauthorized access or interference.
