Ground Control Station

Mobile Command Center for Electric Aviation Operations

Home / Civilian / Ground Control Station

Your Complete Field Operations Hub

The Ground Control Station is a self-contained mobile unit that provides everything needed for Heavy 1 operations in the field. Designed as a towable trailer platform, it serves as battery charging station, mission control center, maintenance workspace, and aircraft hangar -- all in one.

With integrated solar panels, a backup generator, and rapid battery charging capability, the Ground Control Station ensures that your Heavy 1 fleet can operate continuously from any location. No airport infrastructure required. No grid connection needed. Simply tow it to the field and begin operations.

The station is purpose-engineered to support the unique demands of electric agricultural aviation, where fast turnarounds and field-side deployment are essential to maximizing productivity during critical spray windows.

Rapid Battery Charging & Hot-Swap

The Ground Control Station features a high-capacity battery charging system designed for maximum field uptime. While one battery pack powers the Heavy 1 in flight, the next pack charges on the station -- ready for an immediate hot-swap the moment the aircraft returns.

This rapid turnaround capability means the Heavy 1 spends more time in the air treating crops and less time on the ground waiting. Multiple battery packs can be cycled throughout the day, enabling continuous operations from sunrise to sunset without interruption.

The charging system monitors cell health, temperature, and charge cycles to ensure long battery life and safe operation in all conditions.

Integrated Solar Power

Deployable solar panels mounted on the Ground Control Station generate clean, sustainable energy to supplement battery charging in the field. When deployed, the solar array captures energy throughout the day, reducing reliance on the backup generator and further lowering operating costs.

The solar system is designed for rapid deployment and stowage -- panels fold out in minutes when the station is positioned, and retract just as quickly for transport. This means zero setup complexity and immediate power generation upon arrival at the field site.

Redundant Power & Mission Control

A backup generator ensures operations continue regardless of weather or solar conditions. Whether it is overcast skies, early morning starts before sunrise, or extended operations into the evening, the generator provides reliable power to keep battery charging on schedule.

The integrated control interface gives operators complete mission oversight: flight planning, real-time telemetry monitoring, autonomous flight path management, and system diagnostics. Every aspect of Heavy 1 operations can be managed from the station's command console.

Transport & Aircraft Protection

The Ground Control Station doubles as a secure hangar during transport and overnight storage. The Heavy 1 aircraft fits inside the enclosed trailer, protecting it from weather, dust, and unauthorized access while in transit between field sites.

This dual-purpose design eliminates the need for separate transport vehicles and hangar facilities. One truck, one trailer -- and you have everything needed to deploy a complete electric agricultural aviation operation anywhere it is needed.

Deploy Anywhere. Operate Independently.

The Ground Control Station transforms any flat surface into a fully operational airfield. No runways, no hangars, no fuel trucks, no grid power. Just tow the station to your fields, deploy the solar panels, load the batteries, and start flying. The entire system is engineered for self-sufficiency, enabling agricultural operations in remote areas where traditional ag aviation infrastructure simply does not exist.

From large commercial farms to remote rural properties, the Ground Control Station and Heavy 1 combination delivers professional aerial application services without the overhead of fixed-base operations. This is the future of agricultural aviation -- mobile, electric, and completely field-ready.
